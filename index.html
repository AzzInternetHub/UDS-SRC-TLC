About The SRC

The Students' Representative Council (SRC) of the University for Development Studies, TLC, serves as the ultimate democratic vehicle and collective voice of the student body. We bridge communication between management and students, protecting welfare while engineering development.

Brief History of UDS

Established to blend academic training with intensive practical fieldwork, UDS remains Ghana's premier development-focused university, driving transformation through community-inserted training paradigms.
